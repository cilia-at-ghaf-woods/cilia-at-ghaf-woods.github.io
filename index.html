Cilia at Ghaf Woods Payment Plan

At Cilia by Majid Al Futtaim, we understand that purchasing a home is a significant investment, which is why we offer flexible payment options tailored to suit your financial needs. Our payment plans are designed to make owning a home in Ghaf Woods more accessible and manageable, allowing you to secure your dream residence with ease.

Down Payment: To reserve your unit at Cilia, we require a down payment that serves as your initial investment. This down payment amount is typically a percentage of the total purchase price and is due upon signing the sales agreement.

Installment Payments: Our payment plans include flexible installment options spread out over the construction period, allowing you to pay for your home in convenient installments that suit your budget. These installment payments are typically linked to construction milestones, ensuring that your payments are aligned with the progress of your property.

Completion Payment: The final payment, known as the completion payment, is due upon the completion of your unit at Cilia. This payment signifies the finalization of your purchase and the handover of your keys, allowing you to move into your new home and start enjoying the Cilia lifestyle.

Assistance from Sales Team: Our dedicated sales team is here to assist you every step of the way, providing guidance and support throughout the purchasing process. Whether you have questions about the payment plan or need assistance with financing options, our team is committed to helping you make informed decisions.

No Hidden Fees: Our payment plans are transparent and straightforward, with no hidden fees or surprises. We believe in honesty and integrity in all our transactions, ensuring that you have a clear understanding of your financial obligations from the outset.
